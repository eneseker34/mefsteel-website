Light Steel Home Prices 2026: Realistic Cost Analysis

"How much does a light steel home cost?" is the first question we hear from every customer. In this article we share 2026's current prices openly, package by package, compare them with reinforced concrete, and explain the factors that affect the price.

💰

Factors That Affect Price

The price of a light steel home is not a single number. The following factors directly determine the final cost:

  • Project size (m²): The unit cost decreases for larger projects. The per-m² price can vary by 15-25% between 100 m² and 300 m².
  • Number of floors: A single-story building is the most economical option. Each additional floor increases cost due to transfer beams and floor systems.
  • Chosen package: The difference between shell construction, advanced shell, and turnkey can be 2-3 times per m².
  • Land conditions: Sloped land, hard ground, or plots with difficult vehicle access create additional cost.
  • Profile choice: Material cost differs by roughly 3 times between a C90/0.9mm profile and a C200/2.5mm profile.
  • Location and shipping: Shipping and accommodation costs are added for regions far from our factory.

💡 The Real Difference: Time Cost

While reinforced concrete takes 12-18 months, a light steel structure is delivered in 3-5 months. During that time you are not paying rent, and your loan accrues less interest. When calculated financially, light steel is generally 15-25% more advantageous.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the foundation included?

Our shell construction package includes the foundation (raft or strip footing). The foundation type is determined based on the soil survey results. Additional cost may arise in unusual soil conditions (marsh, fill, rock, etc.).

Is structural engineering calculated separately and charged extra?

All our packages include a structural calculation report compliant with TBDY 2026. The engineering project needed for the permit process can be provided separately if required.

When are prices updated?

Material prices depend on steel and galvanizing parity. Quotes are valid for 30 days from the date issued. Always call us for the current price.

Is there a payment plan?

Yes. Our standard payment plan follows a progress-payment model: 50% advance (upon contract signing), with the remaining 50% paid in 10-15% installments according to construction stages. Flexible arrangements are possible for those using bank loans.
