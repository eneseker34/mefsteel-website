Light Steel Home Photos: Real Shots from MefSteel Projects

Most light steel home images circulating online are renders — imaginary designs drawn on a computer. We take a different approach: in this article we share photos only of our real projects, completed and delivered on site. Shots from Tekirdağ, Thrace and the European side of Istanbul.

🏠

Most people researching light steel homes have the same first question in mind: "Are houses really built with this system, or am I just looking at renders?" The answer is simple — as of 2026, MefSteel has completed dozens of residential, commercial and industrial projects turnkey. Below you'll find real shots from these projects. All photos are from our own archive, shared with client approval.

Our Residential Projects

Examples from the detached and twin villa projects we have completed in the Tekirdağ and Thrace region. Each one was built with an engineering report compliant with TBDY 2018 and the 2026 Light Steel Regulation.

Our Commercial Building Projects

The light steel system also offers a fast and economical alternative for commercial-use buildings such as shops, offices and showrooms. Wide-span, flexibly planned structures are delivered in a short time.

From the Manufacturing and Assembly Process

Behind every finished structure lies a process spanning weeks — from foundation prep to steel frame shipment. Two real shots from our sites — the "behind the scenes" of light steel construction.

These photos show that the light steel building system is not just a "concept," but structures that are used and have been handed over today — in Turkey, and especially in Thrace and the European side of Istanbul — by real families and businesses.
