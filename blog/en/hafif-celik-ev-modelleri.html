Light Steel Home Models: Real Projects from Studio to Villa

Holiday house, detached home, two-story, villa… At what scale and type can light steel be built? We compare all the models using photos, area, and budget guidance from MefSteel's real projects.

Answer These Questions Before Choosing a Model

  • How many people in the household? Permanent residence or holiday home?
  • How many m² is the plot, and what is the lot coverage ratio (TAKS)?
  • Single story or two stories? Is there an elderly person or someone with limited mobility?
  • What is the budget? Shell construction or turnkey?

The answers to these questions directly determine the model choice. Below we explain the most common models using real project data.

Model 1: Small House / Holiday Home (35-65 m²)

This is the scale where light steel is applied most efficiently. Production and assembly speed are highest, and cost per m² is lowest in this group.

FeatureValue
Typical area35 – 65 m²
Room layoutStudio or 1+1
UseHoliday home, single-person house, guest house, office
Delivery time30 – 45 days (shell)
Shell construction cost550,000 – 900,000 TL
Turnkey cost1,100,000 – 1,600,000 TL

Modular design is popular at this scale: standard 3×6 m or 3×9 m modules can be combined to reach the desired size.

Model 2: 2+1 Detached House (80-115 m²) — Most Popular Choice

This is by far the most frequently built model in MefSteel projects. It offers ample space for a family of four while striking the best balance between budget and comfort.

Our 98 m² 2+1 project completed in Süleymanpaşa, Tekirdağ: shell + roof + facade delivered in 45 days, turnkey delivery in 85 days. The client received it at a cost 22% lower than the reinforced-concrete quote.

Model 3: Large Family Home 3+1 (120-165 m²)

This size is preferred by families of 5 or more who also want a guest room. It can be planned as single-story or 1.5-story (with an attic-level used as living space).

FeatureValue
Typical area120 – 165 m²
Room layout3+1 or 3+2
UseLarge family, countryside house
Delivery time60 – 75 days (shell), 90 – 110 days (turnkey)
Shell construction cost1,400,000 – 2,100,000 TL
Turnkey cost2,800,000 – 4,000,000 TL

Model 4: Two-Story House (160-240 m²)

For projects with a small plot but a large space requirement, a two-story solution is ideal. In light steel construction, the floor between stories is built as a composite system with steel joists, and seismic design is performed according to TBDY 2018.

FeatureValue
Typical area160 – 240 m² (2 stories total)
Room layout4+1, 4+2 or custom plan
Ground floorLiving room, kitchen, guest bathroom
Upper floorBedrooms, bathroom, study
Delivery time75 – 100 days (shell), 110 – 130 days (turnkey)
Turnkey cost3,500,000 – 5,500,000 TL
🛠️ Technical Note

In two-story buildings, the stairwell opening, story height, and floor continuity are critical details. Light steel floor joists are also checked for vibration. MefSteel documents these calculations in the structural report for every project.

Model 5: Villa (200-300 m²)

For large-plot projects, light steel villas are increasingly popular among clients who want high ceilings and large glass surfaces. A villa of the same quality in reinforced concrete costs significantly more.

FeatureValue
Typical area200 – 300 m²
Room layout4+1, 5+1 or custom architecture
Roof typeGable, hip, or flat terrace
Standout featureLarge glass facades, high ceilings, open floor plan
Delivery time90 – 120 days (shell), 130 – 160 days (turnkey)
Turnkey cost5,000,000 – 9,000,000 TL

Model 6: Commercial Building (Office, Warehouse, Showroom)

Light steel is used not only for housing but also for commercial buildings such as offices, showrooms, warehouses, and workshops. Large spans and short assembly time are the standout advantages in this segment.

  • Office: 150-500 m² open office layout, glass facade
  • Showroom: 200-800 m², high ceilings, wide door openings
  • Warehouse / workshop: Climate control, insulation, high ceilings

Frequently Asked Questions

Which model should I choose?

If your family has 2-3 people, a single-story 2+1 is ideal; for 4-5 people, a 3+1 or two-story house works better. If your plot is small, consider two stories. If your budget is tight, you can start with shell construction and complete the interior work in stages.

Can I fully customize the floor plan myself?

Yes. Thanks to the modular production system in light steel construction, custom plan flexibility is high. Window and door positions, room sizes, terraces, and porches are designed according to the client's wishes. The only constraint is compatibility with the structural calculations.

Can a light steel house have a basement?

Technically yes, but it is built with reinforced concrete. The basement floor is not built with light steel but with concrete; the light steel frame sits on top. The decision depends on soil conditions.

Is there a catalog of standard plans?

MefSteel has reference plan templates. These are used as a starting point and customized for each client. We don't sell directly "off the shelf" from a catalog — every project is designed according to the plot and the client's needs.
